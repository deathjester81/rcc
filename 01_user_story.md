## User Story – RCC Owner

As the RCC owner,
I want to open a customer and instantly see:
- where the customer stands in our roadmap
- what has already been delivered
- which RCC modules are available but not yet purchased
so that I can clearly explain our value.

## User Story – Customer

As a customer,
I want to see:
- my current project status
- completed and active phases
- available RCC services
and have an easy way to contact RCC if I want to continue.
